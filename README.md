## What is Ketamine HCl Liquid?
Ketamine HCl (hydrochloride) liquid is a form of the anesthetic drug ketamine, often administered intravenously or intramuscularly in clinical environments. Discovered in the 1960s, it has been used for pain management, sedation, and treatment-resistant depression. In 2026, its applications have expanded under strict medical supervision, including FDA-approved therapies for mental health.

Ketamine works by blocking NMDA receptors in the brain, providing rapid antidepressant effects and dissociation. However, it's classified as a Schedule III controlled substance in the US, meaning it requires a prescription due to potential for abuse and side effects like hallucinations, elevated blood pressure, and dependency.
